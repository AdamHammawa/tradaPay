What's different about us?


Seamless Transactions For Individuals and Organisations.


TradaPay connects Merchants to their Customers & enables quick secured payments

Trada Pay would serve as an intermediary between Merchants and their customers allowing you easily receive money into your Multiple Trada accounts or other banks

As a Customer,you can easily pay your Bills,purchase Products and Pay for Services using any of the available methods of Transfers, TradaMints or by simply selecting the Merchants profile with TradaPay

Tradapay provides a safe, easy, and flexible way for Individuals and Businesses to transact online. Our goal is to help individuals and smart businesses succeed by providing a trusted and reliable payment platform.

With TradaPay, network/internet fluctuations are not a barrier when making payments; our contactless payment options (bluetooth/wifi) are there to make payments flexible and seamless

By integrating our solution into their products, these Businesses can offer their customers a secure and convenient way to make payments and reducing the risk of non-payment or fraud.

TradaMint allows easy generation of code to enable gifting of money

TradaMint allows Users to gift people moneyby geneerating a gift cards called MINT. This can be redeemed on the App by anyone who has the barcode or pin .

As a User sending Mints ,you only has to confirm the transaction. As a Receiver you only have to enter the unique code sent to you and just like that!!,your account is credited.

TradaContactless allows easy Transaction With Users Within your range with A Swipe

TradaContactless allows Users to send and receive money with a swipe when connected to other users within a distance.

You no longer have to request Account details to Transact, just scan and select the Receiver's avatar and with a swipe your transactions are processed

When shopping in Stores,You no longer have to worry about Card or Cash Payments , with the Contactles payment you can scan generated invoice and Swipe to initiate payements.

How can I get started?



Get Onboard With These Simple and Clear Steps


For Individuals

Create / Sign in to Account

Enter your Email address and enter OTP received in your email. Proceed to enter your name and create Password. Now you can log into the App with your Email and Password.

Update Profile

You would be required to provide us with relevant means of Identification for KYC (Know Your Customer) inorder to Activate Account Transactions

Enjoy The App

Now you have Access to Endless Possibility in handling your finance.

For Organisations

Set Up Business/organisation profile

You can easily add a Business Account to your existing TradaPay Account and switch between profiles. On your Business profile, you would be required to provide your Organisation Email and name for separate Login in. Now,update your Business profile with required means of Business identification.

Set Up Your Payment Methods

Once you have update your Organization Profile with TIN and relevant means of Business Identity, you can now choose your preferred method for receiving payment from your customers such as cards, e-wallets, and bank transfers.

Track and Monitor Business Performance

Not only do you get to receive payments easily, you can track your business growth with graphical breakdown and payment references.
